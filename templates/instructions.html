Annotation guidelines

You will be provided with an article and a question. On the basis of the article, please answer if the answer is True or False. In addition, select and erase all sentences/phrases of the document that may help in answering the question. That is, after you are finished erasing there should not be enough information to answer the question either way. To erase a span of text, highlight it and then click “Erase Span”. Once you are done, click Submit. You will be shown a survey code that you should enter in your AMT interface.
